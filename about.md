# Level Swipe
by [undefined0](user:13351341)

---

Scrolling through levels too boring? This mod simply lets you scroll sideways through levels instead of having to exit and go back to the level list every time!

This mod works for all pages where levels are presented in a list (the Featured tab, quick search filters, Map Packs, lists from mods), but will not work for gauntlets (though support is planned).
